THE MISSION

Nalu.com is a website for surfers and non-surfers alike who love the sport of surfing and the culture that surrounds it. This website provides an interactive way for you to learn more about how the sport of surfing has evolved over the years and how innovations in surf photography have influenced some of those changes. Nalu is an educational site with a documentary feel – communicating content through beautiful photography, infographics, and videos to provide a fun, informative, and interactive experience.
